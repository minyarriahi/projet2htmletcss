Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper until they are well mixed and have a uniform color.
  2. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until melted.
  3. Pour the eggs into the skillet. Tilt the skillet to spread the eggs evenly.
  4. Cook the omelette. As the eggs begin to set, gently push the edges towards the center using a spatula, allowing uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  5. Add fillings. Once the eggs are mostly set, add your desired fillings to one side of the omelette.
  6. Fold the omelette. Carefully fold the omelette in half with a spatula and cook for an additional minute or until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked.
  7. Serve immediately. Slide the omelette onto a plate and enjoy!
